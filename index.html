About This Teacher Pack

Welcome to the Live Well Reproductive and Sexual Health, Second Edition , Teacher's Guide.

Congratulations! You have complete access to the teacher resources that will help you deliver the Live Well Reproductive and Sexual Health program.

This Teacher's Guide contains all the tools you need to easily implement a flexible, customizable program, including eight lesson plans that correspond to the lessons in the interactive web text or ebook. You will be able to tailor the program to the needs of your students and your school.

Each lesson of Live Well Reproductive and Sexual Health comes with supporting ancillaries for students and teachers. These materials are designed to progress student learning in a way that builds critical health skills grounded in functional health knowledge. Each lesson has a corresponding lesson planning chart with links to all associated instructor and student resources. Some elements are editable and should be customized to fit your class. For example, the optional Understanding My Health preassessment is a simple questionnaire where students can answer Yes or No to determine what they already know and what they don't. You may wish to remove certain items from the preassessment if you will not be teaching them, such as "I can explain what sexual orientation is" or "I can explain what gender identity means." Many materials are modifiable to allow teachers to make adjustments that best suit the characteristics and readiness of their students, the unique and nuanced standards and expectations of their school districts and states, and the specific strengths of the teachers themselves.

To begin, simply choose How to Use the Teacher Pack.
